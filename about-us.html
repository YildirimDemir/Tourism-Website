About Us

WHO ARE WE ? Founded in Istanbul in 2013, our company first started by making hotel reservations for holidaymakers in the local sector. He then proved himself in this field after raising his reputation and establishing close relations with hotels. Then we decided to expand the business and open up to the global, of course, we still added something to ourselves locally. We introduced the world to people with world, country and city tours. We offer privileges such as hotel reservations and private car allocation for holidaymakers. We have agreements with various transportation companies so that our customers travel with the highest level of comfort and interest. We continue to grow non-stop. Finally, in talks with Elon Musk, we agreed to organize tours to the planet Mars.
