Required Documents

  • Valid Passport: A valid passport is essential for international travel, including a world tour on a cruise ship. Ensure that your passport is valid for at least six months beyond the duration of your trip.
  • Visa(s): Depending on the countries you plan to visit during your world tour, you may need to obtain visas in advance. Research the visa requirements for each destination and apply for the necessary visas well ahead of your departure date.
  • Travel Insurance: It is highly recommended to have comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies, trip cancellation or interruption, lost luggage, and other unforeseen circumstances. Review different insurance options and choose one that best suits your needs.
  • Health Documents: Some countries may require specific health documents, such as proof of vaccination or a yellow fever certificate. Check the health requirements of your destinations and consult with a healthcare professional or travel clinic to ensure you have the necessary vaccinations and documents.
  • Cruise Ticket and Itinerary: Once you have booked your world tour cruise, you will receive a cruise ticket and itinerary. Carry a printed or digital copy of these documents, as they will serve as proof of your booking and provide details about your cruise ship, ports of call, and embarkation/disembarkation dates and locations.
